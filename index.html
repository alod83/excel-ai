About the book

From formulas and prompts to dashboards and Python

What you will learn

The book shows how to combine Excel with generative AI to speed up repetitive tasks, sharpen analysis, and make advanced workflows more approachable.

Tools covered

ChatGPT, Microsoft Copilot, Excel AI add-ins, and Python workflows all appear in practical, workbook-centered examples.

Who it is for

Analysts, Excel power users, data practitioners, and curious teams who want to turn AI into a useful daily habit instead of a vague promise.

Inside the chapters

A practical path through the Excel AI landscape

Part I

Using ChatGPT in Excel

Learn prompt structure, formula help, template creation, data enrichment, and troubleshooting through conversational workflows.

Part II

Data Science with Copilot

Move from data preparation to statistics, visualization, dashboards, and reporting with Copilot directly inside Excel.

Part III

Other AI tools for Excel

Explore add-ins, OpenAI-powered workflows, and Python-based extensions for more specialized use cases.

Part IV

Stories and solutions

Finish with applied fiction and activity solutions that turn technical learning into something memorable and hands-on.
