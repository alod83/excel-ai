Applied Fiction

When story becomes part of the learning system

A narrative approach that turns analytical tasks into the engine of progression.

Story + Analysis + Progression

Definition

What applied fiction is

Applied fiction is a narrative design approach in which fiction is not just used to decorate a lesson or introduce a topic. Instead, the story becomes a functional part of the learning experience. The fictional world, the datasets, and the analytical tasks are designed together, so that progress in the narrative depends on the learner’s ability to observe evidence, interpret information, and complete meaningful problem-solving steps. In this model, story and analysis move together: the reader is not simply told what happens next, but earns the next step by thinking through the data.

Why it works

A bridge between engagement and data reasoning

Applied fiction works because it gives analytical reasoning both context and momentum. A fictional setting gives the learner a reason to care about the questions being asked and the evidence being explored, while each analytical step becomes part of a meaningful progression. Cleaning data, recognizing patterns, interpreting charts, and testing anomalies no longer feel like isolated exercises. They become connected actions inside a larger sequence, making the learning experience more engaging, more memorable, and easier to follow from one discovery to the next.

Story trailer

Plot

High in the shadow of Everest, something has gone wrong. Signals do not align, fragments of information raise new questions, and what first looks like a difficult expedition begins to feel like a mystery that can only be understood through careful analysis. To move forward, raw data must be cleaned, patterns must be recognized, anomalies must be tested, and scattered clues must be turned into evidence. Excel becomes the investigation room. AI becomes a thinking partner. And every table, chart, and insight brings the reader one step closer to understanding what is really happening on the mountain. The stakes are real, the atmosphere grows tighter with every discovery, and the truth stays just out of reach long enough to keep the next question alive.
